About Us

We are Emirates Airline Reservation System, the leading airline in the world, providing premium travel experiences to millions of passengers every year.

Our mission is to connect people and cultures, and to make the world a smaller, more interconnected place. With a fleet of over 250 aircraft and flights to over 150 destinations, we are committed to providing exceptional service and creating unforgettable memories for our passengers.

Thank you for choosing Emirates for your travel needs. We look forward to welcoming you on board.
